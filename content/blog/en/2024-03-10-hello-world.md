It is with great enthusiasm that I launch this space. My name is José Pedro, a passionate software engineer and tireless researcher, always seeking new horizons in the field of technology. On this blog I will share my discoveries, projects and, of course, some code.

**My Journey**

I became interested in technology in high school thanks to the school labs that used Linux, and soon I was already playing at being a programmer. I decided to pursue a degree in technology, and from the beginning I researched the development of educational technologies. After graduation I completed a master's degree and after some time teaching and working in industry, I recently started a PhD in Informatics in Education, an area where I work as a Tech Lead in an EdTech company.

**What to expect from this blog?**

This site will serve as a platform to share not only my work and projects, but also insights, tutorials and discussions about the latest trends and challenges in technology. Here, you will find:

- **Posts:** Reflections on my journey, lessons learned and the challenges I've faced.
- **Tutorials and Guides:** From quick tips to in-depth guides on programming, frameworks, tools and much more.
- **Research:** I'll share insights about my research in the area of educational technology.

**An Invitation**

I consider this first post not just an introduction, but an invitation. An invitation for you to join me on this journey of discovery, learning and creation. There's a universe of possibilities out there, and I can't wait to explore it with you.

So, if you're ready to embark on this adventure with me, feel free to explore, read, learn and interact. Let's together unravel the mysteries of technology and create something truly incredible.

See you soon,

Zé
